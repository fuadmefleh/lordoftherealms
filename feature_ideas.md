# Feature Ideas for Lord of the Realms

Here are several feature ideas designed to enhance gameplay depth, immersion, and fun without requiring immediate code implementation.

## üåç World & Immersion
- **Dynamic Seasons & Weather**: 
    - Visual changes (snow in winter, blooming in spring).
    - Gameplay impacts: Reduced movement in winter, crop yield bonuses in autumn.
- **Wildlife & Ecosystems**:
    - Herds of animals that migrate.
    - Hunting mechanics for food/resources.
    - Dangerous predators in specific biomes.
- **Natural Wonders**:
    - Unique, rare landmarks (e.g., "The Crystal Caves", "Giant's Causeway") that provide unique buffs or renown when discovered.

## üëë Politics & Diplomacy
- **Dynastic Systems**:
    - Marriages between kingdoms to forge strong alliances.
    - Inheritance disputes and civil wars upon a ruler's death.
- **Espionage Network**:
    - Recruit spies to steal technology, incite rebellion, or assassinate key targets.
    - "Fog of War" lifting for specific enemy regions.
- **Vassalage & Tributes**:
    - Ability to demand tribute from weaker neighbors instead of conquering them.
    - Becoming a vassal to a stronger kingdom for protection.

## üí∞ Economy 2.0
- **Trade Guilds**:
    - Join or found guilds (Merchant's Guild, Blacksmith's Guild) for exclusive perks.
    - Guild missions and reputation.
- **Dynamic Resource Prices**:
    - Scarcity-driven pricing (e.g., grain is expensive during a drought).
    - "Gold Rushes" when new resource nodes are discovered.
- **Auctions & Black Market**:
    - Bid on rare items or artifacts.
    - Smuggle illegal goods for high profit but high risk.

## ‚öîÔ∏è Military & Combat
- **Tactical Formations**:
    - Rock-paper-scissors unit counters (Cavalry > Archers > Pikemen > Cavalry).
    - Terrain bonuses (Archers on hills, Cavalry on plains).
- **Hero Units**:
    - Powerful unique units with skill trees (Generals, Battlemages).
    - Can equip legendary items.
- **Siege Mechanics**:
    - Construct siege engines (trebuchets, battering rams) to take fortified cities.
    - Starvation tactics for long sieges.

## üìú Quests & Adventure
- **Procedural Dungeons**:
    - Ruins, caves, and keeps generated on the map.
    - Risk vs. reward exploration for loot.
- **Legendary Artifacts**:
    - Unique items hidden in the world (e.g., "Sword of the First King").
    - Quests to reforge broken artifacts.
- **Reputation-Based Questlines**:
    - Unique quests for being "Honorable" vs "Cruel".
    - Kingdom-specific storylines.

## üè∞ Base Building & Customization
- **Player Stronghold**:
    - A customizable castle or estate.
    - Upgrade wings (Library for research, Barracks for training).
- **Settlement Specialization**:
    - Designate towns as "Mining Towns", "Trade Hubs", or "Fortresses" for specialized bonuses.
- **Statues & Monuments**:
    - Build monuments to commemorate victories or high renown.
