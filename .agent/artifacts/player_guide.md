## Daily Routine

### Efficient Day Structure:
1. **Morning:** Check notifications for completed caravans/contracts
2. **Travel:** Move to new location (settlement or resource)
3. **Actions:** Trade, recruit, preach, or build
4. **Evening:** Start caravan or accept contract if available
5. **End Day:** Collect income, pay upkeep, spread faith

### Resource Management:
- **Gold:** Primary currency for everything
- **Karma:** Religious currency for miracles
- **Stamina:** Movement points (10 per day)
- **Army Upkeep:** Daily cost to maintain units

## Pro Tips

### Economic:
- Build properties near resource nodes for bonus income
- Level 1 properties are cost-effective, level 5 are powerful
- Prosperity blessing (15 karma) doubles property income for 5 days
- Caravans are profitable but require upfront investment

### Military:
- Knights are expensive but worth it (30 strength each)
- Always keep enough gold for upkeep or lose units
- Risky contracts pay more but may cause casualties
- Level up units through easy contracts first

### Religious:
- Monasteries give +2 karma/day (passive karma generation)
- Preach at large cities for maximum follower gain
- Save karma for Prosperity blessing (best ROI)
- Temples spread faith automatically (passive followers)

### General:
- Visit settlements often (trade, recruit, preach all available)
- Don't overextend (keep emergency gold reserve)
- Balance all three paths for maximum power
- Watch for kingdom wars (opportunities for contracts)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Spending all gold:** Always keep 200-500 reserve
2. **Ignoring upkeep:** Army without gold = desertion
3. **Building too fast:** Properties need time to pay off
4. **Forgetting to preach:** Free followers at every settlement
5. **Not using miracles:** Karma is meant to be spent
6. **Overextending army:** Quality > quantity
7. **Ignoring skills:** They provide huge bonuses
8. **Not diversifying:** Use all three paths
