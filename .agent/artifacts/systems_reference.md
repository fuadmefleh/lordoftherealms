## Settlement Types & Production

### Capital
- Base: 50 gold, 100 food, 30 goods per day
- Population: 5,000-15,000
- Highest production rates

### City
- Base: 30 gold, 60 food, 20 goods per day
- Population: 1,000-5,000
- Strong production

### Town
- Base: 15 gold, 30 food, 10 goods per day
- Population: 500-3,000
- Moderate production

### Village
- Base: 5 gold, 15 food, 3 goods per day
- Population: 50-500
- Basic production

## Diplomatic Relations

### Relation Values
- **+70 to +100**: Alliance possible
- **+20 to +69**: Friendly
- **-19 to +19**: Neutral
- **-69 to -20**: Unfriendly
- **-100 to -70**: Hostile (war likely)

### Relation Changes
- Relations change randomly by -5 to +10 each turn
- War sets relations to -100
- Alliances require +70 relations
- Alliances break below +30 relations

## War & Combat

### War Declaration
- Requires relations < -30
- Attacker must have 80%+ of defender's strength OR 10% random chance

## Economy Tips

### Maximizing Production
1. **Settle near resources**: Resources within 2 hexes provide bonuses
2. **Multiple resources stack**: Nearby resources give 50% of their bonus
3. **Population matters**: Larger populations produce more
4. **Trade routes**: Establish routes between settlements for extra gold

## Watching the World

### What to Look For
- **Notifications**: Show wars, alliances, conquests
- **Territory colors**: Watch kingdoms expand/contract
- **Settlement info**: Click to see economic health
- **Kingdom panel**: Double-click territory to see full kingdom status

### Signs of a Healthy Kingdom
- Growing population
- Multiple trade routes
- Positive relations with neighbors
- Expanding territory
- Strong military

### Signs of a Struggling Kingdom
- Shrinking population (food shortage)
- No trade routes
- Many enemies
- Losing territory in wars
- Weak military
